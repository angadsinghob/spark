The Food donation Drive

There are people who are facing hunger in your community. Hunger exists in every state and county in India. Your local food banks, shelters, and soup kitchens rely on the donations they receive from people like you to help keep their shelves stocked. Food drives are also a great way to raise awareness about hunger and inspire others to take action.

₹1,750 will provide 2 meals a day for a week to one family. And ₹500 can help a poor family with one week's ration. Meals will be served to hungry families in need across the country. Each meal will be a nutritional platter comprising healthy staples like rice, daal, vegetables, chapati and more.
